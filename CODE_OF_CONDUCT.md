# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Alcove to be a calm, constructive place to collaborate. Contributors should aim to be respectful, direct, and generous with context.

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- giving actionable, kind feedback
- assuming good intent while still being honest about problems
- making space for learners and first-time contributors
- documenting decisions that affect others

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- personal attacks or repeated bad-faith engagement
- publishing private information without permission
- hostile or demeaning conduct in issues, discussions, reviews, or chat

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that harms the community or makes collaboration unsafe.

## Reporting

If something happens that should be reported privately, contact the maintainers through the repository's private contact path once that is published. Until then, avoid public escalation of personal information.
